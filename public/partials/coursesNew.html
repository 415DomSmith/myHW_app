To find your Google Calendar code, visit Google Calendars. On the left sidebar, you will see a list of your existing calendars. Click on the calendar that you would like to use for this course, then select “Calendar Settings”.

Find the "Embed This Calendar" portion of the page. Copy and paste the code found in the scrollable text area box (look for the word "iframe" and copy everything in that box). Now paste what you found into the box above.


While you're on this calendar settings page, also find the "Calendar Address" section. Click on "Change sharing settings". Here you can either make the calendar public or allow access to individual people. Don't worry, students will be able to copy this calendar into their own Google calendars, but wont be able to change or affect your calendar in any way.
