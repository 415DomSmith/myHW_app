About

my_HW uses your Google account to allow you to bring in files from Google Drive and assign and share them with your students.

Create assignments, attach documents to assignments, and set due dates. Students can submit answers to assignments directly through my_HW, or submit a link to a document on their Google drive.

Teachers can grade students' assignments, track the number of students that have turned in their homework or response to an assignment, and track a students performance through my_HW.

Teachers also have the power to manage and share classroom specific Google calendars, so students always know what is going on in the classroom!
